Cyberglow

Cyberglow is a top-down shooter which contains flashy lights and feels fun to play.
Cyberglow is a game which was quickly made in a day worth of time.
The main point of the game is to take simple shapes, and making a well polished game out of it.
The game mainly focuses on gamefeel, such as screenshake,
feedback of when you kill enemies and feedback when you obtain more score.
The enemies makes use of the A* pathfinding algorithm to find the player.
