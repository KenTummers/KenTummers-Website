Road to Something

Road to Something is a platformer where you start of with absolutely nothing.
Road to Something is a game made for Ludum Dare 45.
Ludum Dare is a gamejam competition, where participants have to complete a game in 48 hours.
The participants have to create everything from scratch, such as code, art and music.
This was my first time participating in a gamejam.
