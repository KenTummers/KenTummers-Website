Dave's Birthday Party

In Dave's Birthday Party you get invited to Dave's birthday party, but there is a twist.
You live on a mountain, while Dave lives all the way down on the ground.
Dave's Birthday Party is a game made for Ludum Dare 46.
Ludum Dare is a gamejam competition, where participants have to complete a game in 48 hours.
The participants have to create everything from scratch, such as code, art and music.
Ken Tummers
